Hampshire Motorcycles Service and Aftersales Department

We have a dedicated team of manufacturer-trained technicians, who offer the utmost friendly and professional service. Each technician attends regular training to ensure they are fully up to date with specifications of each manufacturer, so you can rely on us to maintain your motorcycle keeping it in peak condition. With the recent workshop refurbishment, each Technician now has state of the art work bays with efficiently designed benches and tool cabinets to house their extensive range of tools. We quality control all of our work to strict policies which enables us to pride ourselves on the exceptional level of service we provide. As an Insurance Approved repairer, we provide a free estimation service, so if you have recently had an accident and are seeking a quotation, don’t hesitate to contact us. Our services include:

  • Servicing
  • Fully equipped Dyno specialist
  • Major engine re-builds
  • Dedicated diagnostic technicians
  • Fault Diagnosis
  • Rapid-bike installation
  • Insurance repairs undertaken
  • Road/Track/Race specialist
  • Collection & delivery
  • Dyno runs and Performance setups
  • Race preparation and maintenance
  • Tracker approved installer
